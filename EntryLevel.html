1853 Stockholm Cholera Outbreak

The 1853 Stockholm cholera outbreak was a severe outbreak of cholera that occurred in Stockholm, Sweden in 1853 as part of the third cholera pandemic.
The dataset for this analysis as provided by Quantum Analytics NG was collected from hand-written church records. The church was responsible for all population registration until around 1870.

The dataset was in Swedish language and was translated to English for better understanding using Google Translate.
