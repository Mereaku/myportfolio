Moulin Pooch Co.: Financial Performance FY21

Moulin Pooch Co. sells 10 different products distributed through 17 stores that range from vets, to online pet shops, physical pet shops, community clubs that bulk buy and individual resellers.

The task involves analyzing Moulin Pooch Co.'s financial data, creating charts for trends, identifying top-performing stores and assessing Dr Paws transactions.
A competitor analysis of Mad Paws is included, covering financial statement comparisons and ratio calculations. The task also involves loan repayment calculations and evaluating two investment opportunities for Moulin Pooch Co.

The ultimate goal is to provide recommendations based on the financial insights.
